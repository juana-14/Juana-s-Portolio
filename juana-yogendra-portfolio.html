03 / Case studies

SmartCare Navigator

Group capstone — dashboard & data lead
Power BI Salesforce Health Cloud
Problem

Australian patients navigate GPs, pharmacies, telehealth and hospitals with no connected system, contributing to ED overcrowding and inconsistent care.

Approach

Built the Power BI analytics layer for an AI-enabled care navigation platform — cleaned and standardised healthcare datasets, then designed KPI cards, trend lines and executive summaries tracking ED visits, wait times, telehealth adoption and satisfaction.

Impact

35% improvement in headline KPIs modelled after rollout; non-urgent ED visits trending down and patient satisfaction holding between 86–95% across the year.

Climate Risk Dashboard — Rockhampton Flood Resilience

Group capstone — dashboard build & strategy sections
Tableau Climate Data
Problem

How might Rockhampton Regional Council and energy providers use climate data to prepare for flooding risk to critical infrastructure — including the Stanwell Future Energy Hub and regional wind and solar assets?

Approach

Built a three-part Tableau suite — Flood Risk, Rainfall and Temperature dashboards — from 15 years of historical climate data (2010–2024), covering flood frequency, regional risk comparison and heatwave trends. Authored the Energy Infrastructure and strategy sections of the proposal.

Impact

Delivered differentiated recommendations — high-risk-area prioritisation for Council, infrastructure hardening for energy providers — plus a costed rollout timeline and early-warning system proposal.

FreshMart Customer Analytics

Individual assessment — market share decomposition
Excel Pivot Tables
Problem

FreshMart, a Riverdale grocery retailer, was losing market share despite a prime location — ownership needed to know why, relative to two direct competitors.

Approach

Built pivot tables to decompose market share into penetration and average-spend-per-customer components, then layered in attitudinal survey data across price, assortment and service perception.

Impact

Diagnosed a 21.0% share vs. 41.7% for the market leader, driven by weak price and assortment perception; delivered four prioritised, literature-backed recommendations on pricing, assortment, bundling and loyalty.

Practera — Buyer Persona & Market Intelligence

8-week industry consulting engagement
Market Research Stakeholder Analysis
Problem

A client building an industrial decarbonisation "Centre of Excellence" needed to understand who would actually buy its data intelligence offering, and why.

Approach

Synthesised 20+ industry sources (KPMG, BCG, IEA, DOE, Siemens, McKinsey, IFRS/AASB standards) into four buyer personas spanning Operations, Finance, HR and Insurance — each mapped to KPIs, buying triggers, objections and data needs.

Impact

Delivered a structured go-to-market research pack with an explicit gap analysis, flagging where evidence was thin and recommending how to close it — primary interviews, insurer partnerships, internal pilots.
